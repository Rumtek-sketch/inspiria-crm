Core ERP Modules

10 Functional Modules

From paddy gate to MIS dashboard — every operational area covered and integrated into a single unified system.

Module 01
Procurement & Paddy Purchasing
PO management, farmer/vendor master, moisture & weight capture, grading, GRN, advance payment tracking, and purchase return management.
Purchase Manager
Week 1–3
Module 02
Milling & Production
Milling order management, paddy batch allocation, stage tracking (Pre-cleaning → Husking → Whitening → Grading → Polish), yield calculation, and finished goods posting.
Mill Operations Head
Week 2–4
Module 03
Inventory & Warehouse
Multi-warehouse management for paddy, finished rice, byproducts, and packing materials. FIFO/LIFO valuation, reorder alerts, and physical stock verification.
Warehouse Manager
Week 3–5
Module 04
Sales & Distribution
Customer master, quotation/rate card, SO management, dispatch & delivery order, GST-compliant invoice, e-Invoice/e-Way Bill integration, and debtor aging.
Sales Manager
Week 4–6
Module 05
Accounts & Finance
Full AP/AR/GL accounting, bank reconciliation, cost centre allocation, GST (GSTR-1, GSTR-3B), TDS management, fixed assets, and daily P&L reporting.
Finance Manager
Week 5–7
Module 06
HR & Payroll
Employee master (permanent/contract/daily wage), biometric attendance, payroll with PF/ESI/PT, labour contract management, leave management, and Factories Act compliance.
HR Manager
Week 6–8
Module 07
Quality Control
Paddy intake QC (moisture, foreign matter, broken grain %), in-process stage QC, finished product certification, lab records, COA generation, and QC dashboards.
QC In-charge
Week 4–6
Module 08
Logistics & Transport
Fleet register, trip planning, freight cost management, weighbridge integration, gate pass control, transporter billing, and vehicle turnaround reporting.
Logistics Head
Week 5–7
Module 09
MIS & Reporting Dashboard
CEO/MD daily snapshot, production/procurement/sales/finance dashboards, per-ton cost analysis, byproduct revenue tracker, and WhatsApp/email alert automation.
Operations Director
Week 7–9
Module 10
Compliance & Documentation
Statutory registers (Factories Act, Contract Labour Act), GST returns preparation, TDS challan/certificates, Mandi/APMC compliance, and annual audit trail archival.
Admin/Compliance Head
Week 8–10
Phased Rollout

12-Week Implementation Roadmap

Seven structured phases — each requiring formal sign-off before the next begins. Parallel run mandatory for Finance and Inventory modules.

🔍
PHASE 1 Discovery & Gap Analysis Week 1–2
ERP Consultant + Department Heads
Conduct current-state process mapping and gap identification Scope document sign-off
⚙️
PHASE 2 System Design & Configuration Week 3–4
ERP Team + IT
System architecture, module configuration, UAT environment setup UAT environment ready
📦
PHASE 3 Data Migration Week 4–5
Finance + Warehouse + HR
Vendor/farmer master, customer, item, employee, asset & opening balances Clean master data loaded
🧪
PHASE 4 User Acceptance Testing (UAT) Week 5–7
All Module Owners
Module-wise UAT sign-off · UAT failures resolved within 48 hours UAT sign-off per module
🎓
PHASE 5 Training & Change Management Week 7–8
HR + ERP Consultant
Role-specific hands-on lab training for all user groups Training completion >90%
🚀
PHASE 6 Go-Live & Hypercare Week 9–10
All Teams + ERP Support
2-week parallel run for Finance & Inventory · Floor support by consultant Live transactions stable
📊
PHASE 7 Post Go-Live Review Week 11–12
Operations Director + Finance
KPI review, performance benchmarking, stability confirmation Performance dashboard live
